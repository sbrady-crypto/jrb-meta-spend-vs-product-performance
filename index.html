Data Notes

How to read this.

  • Meta spend is filtered by product keyword in the ad name (miraclebalm, herokit, wtf, jetm, etc.). Campaigns containing returning, viewcontent, or quiz are excluded.
  • New-customer revenue is Shopify net_sales filtered to new_or_returning_customer = 'new'. Orders are orders_first_time.
  • Complexion Set is a bundle: Shopify reports $0 net_sales for the parent SKU because revenue flows to component line items. Revenue here is reconstructed by counting orders that contain the The Complexion Set Tyvek Bag line item and multiplying by the bundle price ($73.50 new / $73.50 total).
  • Shared-product ad names: Miracle Balm vs. Mini Miracle Balm are disambiguated by the mini prefix; Bronzer excludes cocoabronze and pinkybronze (Miracle Balm shades); Mascara excludes minimascara.
  • Classic Lip has zero Meta spend in this window.
  • Launches: Mini Miracle Balm and Eyeshadow Stick ramped Feb 9–Mar 2; Lip Recharge scaled from Mar 16; Blushing Stick launched 2026-04-13. Read short-window deltas with launch context.
  • Period Δ compares the selected window to the equal-length window immediately before it. For L4W with window end 04-20, that's weeks 03-23 → 04-20 vs. 02-23 → 03-22.
  • AMER = Acquisition Media Ratio (new-customer revenue ÷ Meta spend). Excludes LTV, repeat purchases, and cross-sell. A low AMER on a hero product may still be profitable over the customer lifetime — treat as a directional acquisition signal, not final P&L.
